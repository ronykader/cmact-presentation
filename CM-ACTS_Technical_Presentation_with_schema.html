Executive Summary

Why CM‑ACTS

A transparent, secure, and responsive digital channel between the BARMM government and its citizens — centralizing collection, routing, and resolution of citizen concerns.

◆

Decentralized Intake, Centralized Tracking

Citizens submit via the mobile app; routing logic dynamically assigns concerns to the right agency.

✦

Security & Auditability

Every status change, assignment, or reclassification generates a permanent audit trace.

◈

Data Privacy by Design

Citizen personal details are restricted from agency staff unless explicitly authorized.

◉

Multi‑Channel Notifications

Real-time updates via SMS, Email, and Push — coordinated automatically.

New Capability

Agency‑specific guided questions

When a citizen selects a government agency, the mobile app suggests tailored questions — improving submission quality and consistency.

📱 For Citizens

  • Select an agency (e.g., Ministry of Health)
  • App displays active, agency-specific suggested questions
  • Guided prompts lead to clearer, more complete submissions
GET /agencies/{agency_id}/questions

⚙ For Administrators

  • Create new suggested questions per agency
  • Activate or deactivate any suggestion instantly
  • Bulk-reorder the full suggestion list per agency
  • Full CRUD control — no engineering support required

  • full_name, email, phone_number

  • message
